How to use our App

Story Creation

Well to begin you need to create a Story,
it will allow you to have the beginning ground to elaborate your ideas and create chapters and also write commentaries

Chapter Creation

Then you create a chapter, A chapter is composed with an image and a story,
you pick an image in your gallery and write something that goes with it

Image Creation

This part is the most interesting one for me, picking the images that will go to your gallery,
you can either pick one yourself with url you found in the web or an image delivered by our app's gallery
